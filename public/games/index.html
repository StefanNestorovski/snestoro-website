N-Body Simulation (play here)

A simulation that allows you to watch planets orbit eachother and watch them move around and get affected by all the gravitational forces. Made in javascript.

Spacevasion (play here)

A fun little space shooter game where you fly around in space and shoot down enemies. Made in javascript.

PONG (play here)

Just a simple replica of the classic arcade game Pong. Move your paddle with the mouse and try to score against the other player. Made in javascript.

BACTERIO (play here)

An attempt at simulating bacteria growth and spread. Each block represents a bacteria, which can move around, reproduce, eat, kill other bacteria, and die. Same color bacteria have a lower chance of killing each other and the black cricle represent a food source (needed to stay alive). Made in javascript.

FISHY

Another replica of a flash game I enjoyed playing as a child, the goal is to avoid the big fish while trying to catch the little fish. Made in Unity using C#.
